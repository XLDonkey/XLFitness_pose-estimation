What it does

AI-Powered Form Analysis

🪑
Machine Occupancy Detection
Automatically detects when a member sits at a machine using hip and knee position analysis. Sessions start and stop without any button presses.
🔢
Automatic Rep Counting
Tracks joint angles through each rep cycle. Counts only full reps — partial reps are flagged separately, encouraging proper form.
📐
Back Posture Tracking
Monitors spine alignment in real time. Warns when excessive back lean is detected, reducing injury risk and improving technique.
🧠
Per-Machine AI Calibration
Each machine gets its own training profile recorded from real reps. The AI scores form against that machine's specific range of motion.
📊
Form Scoring (0–100)
Every rep is scored across three dimensions: range of motion, back angle, and rep control speed. Instant feedback after each rep.
💬
Live Coaching Cues
Real-time coaching messages guide members: "Pull all the way down", "Keep your back straight", "Extend fully at the top".
How it works

From Camera to Rep Count in Milliseconds

1

Camera captures the user

A standard webcam or IP camera is mounted side-on to each machine. No special hardware required.

2

AI detects 17 body landmarks

TensorFlow MoveNet runs in real time, tracking key joints — shoulders, elbows, wrists, hips, knees — at 30fps.

3

Joint angles are calculated

The system calculates elbow angle, back angle, and other form metrics frame by frame using the detected landmarks.

4

Reps counted and scored

Each angle cycle is compared against the machine's training profile. Reps are counted, scored, and coaching cues delivered instantly.
